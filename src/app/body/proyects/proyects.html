Roll Keeper

(In development)

PHP (API REST) | Angular | AWS | MySQL | JavaScript | HTML | SCSS

Platform aimed at game masters of role-playing games, currently in development, to organize stories, characters, combats, music, and dice rolls, improving session manage ment both online and offline.

  • Campaign editor with enriched text and dynamic events.
  • Page with tables to manage mechanics and turns.
  • Creation of playable characters and customizable NPCs.
  • Music integration to enhance scenes from the editor.
  • Quick online dice rolls to compare stats during the session.
